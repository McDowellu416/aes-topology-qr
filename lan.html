LAN (Local Area Network)

Description:
A Local Area Network connects devices within a limited area such as a building, classroom, or base location. It is commonly used in offices, schools, and military facilities.

✅ Benefits

  • High data transfer speeds
  • Low latency
  • Easy to manage locally
  • Secure within a controlled environment

❌ Drawbacks

  • Limited to small geographic area
  • Infrastructure required (cabling, switches)
  • Failure of central devices can affect users
